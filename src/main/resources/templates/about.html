A brief lapse in time

Panda Show was born from a simple statement, TV shows have taken a huge spot in our lives but with the flow of our lives it's not always easy to catch up. The more shows you watch the more easy it is to get mixed up between seasons, episodes or even shows. Our goal at Panda Show is to help you keep in touch with your shows, whenever, wherever.
